Optimal organisation of storage rooms, ensuring that food is stored according to its group and nature, improves productivity in the kitchen while also reducing hygiene-health risks. It is essential to separate cooked foods or those ready for consumption from those that are still raw and, whenever possible, it is a good idea to assign the zones according to the type of food, in separate cold rooms. It is advisable to have separate cold rooms for different types of foods.
